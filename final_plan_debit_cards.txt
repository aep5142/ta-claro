Locked Formulas
- Primary Active Cards = debit primary active + ATM-only primary active
- Supplementary Active Cards = debit supplementary active + ATM-only supplementary active
- Total Active Cards = debit total active + ATM-only total active
- Total Cards with Operations = debit cards with operations + ATM-only cards with operations
- Total Activation Rate = total cards with operations / total active cards
- Supplementary Rate = supplementary active cards / primary active cards
- Operations per Active Card = operations / total active cards
